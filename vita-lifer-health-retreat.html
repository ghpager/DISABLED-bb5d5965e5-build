vita-life® Health Retreat

The vita-life® Health Retreat is situated within 180,000 square meters of Bali’s most beautiful and ancient natural landscape in Tabanan Hills, Bali. The resort will compromise of 112 luxurious, stand-alone villas and a five-star hotel compromising of 48 all-suite hotel apartments in a pyramid-styled building. The vita-life® Health Retreat’s aim is to help the ‘modern human’ find his or her way back to inner wellbeing and relaxation.

The resort will be much more than a luxurious five-star spa hotel within the beautiful Balinese rice terraces; it will offer the guest nine dedicated health and spa villages inspired by ancient sciences and cultures with a wide variety of exquisite cuisines available including Ayurvedic diets.  Every guest that stays in the vita-life® Health Retreat will have their own personal comprehensive health program that begins before their arrival in Bali and continues long after their visit.

The vita-life® Health Retreat will consist of the following nine distinctly themed villages; European, Balinese, Indian, American-Indian, Atlantic, Japanese, Hawaiian, Moroccan, and Chinese. Each village will be characterized by the architecture, interior décor, restaurant and a boutique spa specializing in healing techniques, medicines and treatments of that ancient culture.

The Retreat Management creates and maintains a database and network of world-renowned health specialists and university departments to ensure the best practice of treatments. All of the treatments offered at the vita-life® Health Retreat are based on the findings of modern science and the wisdom of ancient healing traditions. The Retreat’s Academy will be a meeting place for international experts in ancient healing sciences and will also serve as an educational platform for the Retreat’s own practitioners. The academy will be the centre of intense training courses providing our therapists with different levels of qualifications certifying their expertise in their respective fields.

Guests will also have the opportunity to attend meetings and learn more about the healing sciences that they are most interested in. Furthermore guests can join guided tours through the gardens and learn about the healing effects of the different native plants, herbs, and spices that the retreat uses for therapeutic, medicinal and health enhancing purposes. Finally guests will also learn about the plants used for the retreats own line of branded cosmetics and guest bathroom amenities and toiletries.

Single, double or luxury villas are for purchase at vita-life® Health Retreat and can be fully or partially acquired.  There are a number of benefits being a villa owner including complimentary use of the retreat over an annual period of 6 weeks, with all of the 5-star guest services at your disposal. The retreat will maintain and rent out the villa for the remaining weeks giving you an interesting return on your investment. Additionally as a villa owner you will benefit from special rates on spa treatments including a complimentary annual medical check up.

The vita-life® Health Retreat is about to commence construction and will be completed by 2014 using sensitive site planning and harmonious landscaping.  The resort will be built with the highest ecological standards in mind using fair trade produce from neighboring farmers and other Balinese providers.
